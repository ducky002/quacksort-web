Privacy Statement

QuackSort is built with a strict privacy-first approach. We do not use your personal data, face data, or event photos for analytics, model training, or profiling. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Registration on this website is only used to sync your face enrollment with the desktop app of the event creator who shared your event code. When you upload your selfie, our server stores only your first name, last name, and a face model required for matching.

Access to this enrollment data is restricted to you and the event creator linked to your event code. Your event photos remain with your photographer and do not get moved to QuackSort cloud storage as part of this registration flow.

Unlike subscription-based cloud workflows that require uploading full event libraries, QuackSort delivers core sorting capabilities on local devices, giving you stronger control over your memories and reducing exposure to cloud privacy risks.
